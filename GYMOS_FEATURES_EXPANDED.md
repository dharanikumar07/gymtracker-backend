# 🚀 GymOS: Advanced Feature Roadmap

This document outlines the core and expanded features for the GymOS application, categorized by module.

---

## 🏋️ 1. Workout & Performance Module

### Core Features (Your Ideas)
*   **Weekly Workout Split:** Define your split (e.g., PPL, Upper/Lower, Bro Split).
*   **Daily Routine Planner:** Assign specific exercises to each day of the split.
*   **Today's Logger:** Real-time logging of Sets, Reps, and Weight.
*   **History & Charts:** View progression for specific exercises over time.

### 🌟 Pro Features (Added Ideas)
*   **Personal Record (PR) Vault:** A dedicated screen showing your "All-Time Best" for major lifts (Squat, Bench, Deadlift).
*   **Muscle Recovery Heatmap:** A 3D or 2D body visual showing muscle fatigue based on workout history.
*   **Smart Rest Timer:** Integrated timer with sound/vibration alerts between sets.
*   **Volume Tracking (Tonnage):** Analytics showing total weight moved per session (Sets x Reps x Weight).
*   **Drop-Set & Super-Set Support:** Special logging modes for advanced training techniques.
*   **Form Vault:** Ability to attach a 5-second video clip to an exercise to check your form later.

---

## 💰 2. Expense & Diet Module

### Core Features (Your Ideas)
*   **Daily Diet Expense:** Log what you spend on meals, snacks, and protein.
*   **Gym Membership Tracker:** Log monthly fees and renewal dates.
*   **Monthly Analytics:** Pie charts showing where your fitness money goes.

### 🌟 Pro Features (Added Ideas)
*   **Supplement ROI (Return on Investment):** Calculate the cost-per-serving of your supplements.
*   **Subscription Reminders:** Push notifications 3 days before your gym membership or coaching fee is due.
*   **Bulk vs. Cut Expense Analysis:** Compare how much more you spend on food during a "Bulk" phase vs. a "Cut."
*   **Grocery List Integration:** Automatically generate a shopping list based on your frequent diet expenses.

---

## 📊 3. Overall Analytics Dashboard

*   **Consistency Score:** A "Streak" system (e.g., 4 weeks without missing a leg day).
*   **Financial vs. Physical Correlation:** A chart showing if spending more on supplements actually results in higher PRs.
*   **Training Frequency:** A calendar "Heatmap" showing which days of the month you were most active.

---

## ⚙️ 4. Technical Roadmap (Phase 2)

*   **Dark/Light Mode:** Full UI flexibility (Already Implemented).
*   **CSV/PDF Export:** Generate a "Monthly Progress Report" for your coach.
*   **Cloud Sync:** Ensure data is safe even if the phone is lost.
